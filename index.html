The Beginning...

Founded in 1952 by Tata George Dashe, The Elects Apostolic Church (TEAC) arose during a time when African spirituality was largely misunderstood and disregarded. Tata Dashe, guided by his celestial guardians (abokhokho, angels), recognised the urgent need to reconnect people with their spiritual roots. His vision was to restore the lost bond between individuals and their angels, offering a path to spiritual growth, healing, and enlightenment. The church initially held services on Saturday evenings, allowing followers to honour their other religious practices while deepening their understanding of African spirituality.

Now, as then, Tata Dashe’s teachings and guidance are alive, inspiring generations and providing a lasting connection to divine wisdom. The legacy of TEAC empowers individuals to rediscover their spiritual heritage and fosters a deeper relationship with their celestial guardians. Today, TEAC remains a place of refuge, offering a space where ancient wisdom is embraced, and individuals are uplifted on their spiritual journey.

Our Aim

TEAC aims to support you in recognizing and embracing your spiritual essence, helping you feel confident and connected to your unique spiritual journey. The community fosters a safe, empowering space where members can explore their divine nature and grow together in knowledge and understanding. Another core goal is to deepen the connection between each person and their celestial guardians (angels, o'khokho), offering guidance and a sense of support on their spiritual path. TEAC also strives to be a center of learning, dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich wisdom of African spirituality for future generations.
